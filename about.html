About P.R.I.D.E. Ratings

P.R.I.D.E. Stands for Professor Review, Input Data, Evaluation. What does this mean? This means students are able to review and rate professors based on data and evaluation.

This university-exclusive professor rating system gives students access to other student's reviews of their experience in the classroom. This is where students can post their reviews about their classroom experience to aid other students in determing the classes and professors they best align with. This system is available to all current PNW students on the Hammond and Westville campuses.

Purpose

  • Share students' experience in the classroom
  • Provide data driven reviews of professors teaching styles.
  • Aid in student registration.
